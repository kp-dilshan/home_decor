Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options and costs?

We offer several shipping options within Sri Lanka and internationally. Local delivery costs start from Rs. 500 depending on location and package size. International shipping costs vary by destination and are calculated at checkout. Most local orders arrive within 3-5 business days.

Can I visit your workshop or showroom?

Yes! Our Colombo showroom is open Monday-Friday 9am-6pm and Saturdays 10am-4pm. We'd love to show you our collection in person. For workshop visits (where you can see artisans at work), please contact us in advance to schedule an appointment.

Do you offer customization of products?

Many of our traditional crafts can be customized in terms of size, colors, or specific design elements. Custom orders typically take 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. Contact us with your specific requirements for a quote and timeline.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for unused items in original condition. Custom-made or personalized items cannot be returned. Please contact us to initiate a return and we'll provide instructions. Refunds will be processed within 5 business days after we receive the returned item.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer special wholesale pricing for retailers, interior designers, and businesses purchasing in quantity (minimum order value applies). Please use the wholesale inquiry option in our contact form or email sales@ceylondecor.lk for our wholesale catalog and pricing.
