Celebrating Sri Lankan Heritage

Founded in 2010, Ceylon D√©cor began as a small passion project to preserve and promote Sri Lanka's rich artisanal traditions. What started as a humble workshop in Colombo has grown into a thriving platform connecting master craftsmen with discerning customers worldwide.

Our journey began when our founder, Anjali Perera, discovered the challenges faced by traditional artisans in reaching global markets while visiting rural craft villages. Determined to bridge this gap, she established Ceylon D√©cor with a simple mission: to bring authentic Sri Lankan craftsmanship to homes around the world while ensuring fair wages and sustainable practices for our artisans.

Today, we work with over 200 skilled craftsmen across the island, from mask carvers in Ambalangoda to lacquerware artists in Kandy. Each piece in our collection tells a story of heritage, skill, and cultural significance.

Our Mission

To preserve Sri Lanka's traditional crafts by creating sustainable livelihoods for artisans while offering authentic, high-quality decor pieces that celebrate our cultural heritage. We're committed to ethical sourcing, fair trade practices, and maintaining the integrity of traditional techniques.

Our Vision

To become the global ambassador for Sri Lankan craftsmanship, where every home that appreciates cultural authenticity can own a piece of our island's heritage. We envision a future where traditional crafts thrive alongside modern design, creating a sustainable ecosystem for artisans.
